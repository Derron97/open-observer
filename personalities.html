The 16 Observer Profiles

Explore the sixteen personality types and see what strengths and micro‑practices resonate with you.

Explore Each Type in Depth

INFJ

Insightful, compassionate and visionary; you connect deep meaning with action.

Micro‑practice: Spend a few minutes meditating on a long‑term vision and note one small step towards it.

Learn more about the INFJ personality

INTJ

Strategic, focused and innovative; you plan methodically to achieve your goals.

Micro‑practice: Discuss an idea with someone to get input and adjust your plan accordingly.

Learn more about the INTJ personality

INFP

Idealistic, reflective and empathetic; you seek authenticity and inner harmony.

Learn more about the INFP personality

Micro‑practice: Write a short poem or paragraph capturing an emotion you felt this week.

INTP

Analytical, independent and inventive; you enjoy conceptual problems and understanding systems.

Micro‑practice: Share one of your ideas with someone and notice their perspective.

ISFJ

Loyal, detail‑oriented and nurturing; you create stability and support in communities.

Micro‑practice: Ask for help on a task to balance giving and receiving care.

ISTJ

Reliable, organized and systematic; you ensure things are done thoroughly and correctly.

Micro‑practice: Allow yourself to deviate from your routine once and observe your reaction.

ISFP

Sensitive, artistic and adaptable; you appreciate beauty and live by your values.

Micro‑practice: Spend 10 minutes outdoors and sketch or photograph something that inspires you.

ISTP

Practical, observant and flexible; you like solving problems with logical, hands-on solutions.

Micro‑practice: Take a few minutes to reflect before acting on an impulse.

ENFJ

Empathetic and charismatic; you intuitively understand others and inspire them toward shared goals.

Micro‑practice: Practice active listening with a friend and reflect on how your guidance helped.

ENFP

Enthusiastic, imaginative and value‑driven; you thrive on possibilities and meaningful connections.

Micro‑practice: Set aside time to journal about your ideas and feelings to channel your creative energy.

ENTJ

Strategic, assertive and driven; you naturally organize and lead towards efficiency and results.

Micro‑practice: Delegate a task and observe how letting go improves your perspective.

ENTP

Quick‑witted, curious and resourceful; you enjoy exploring new concepts and challenging norms.

Micro‑practice: Pick one routine and tweak it creatively, noting how change sparks new insights.

ESFJ

Warm, conscientious and cooperative; you build harmonious environments and care for others.

Micro‑practice: Say 'no' once this week to prioritize your own needs and see how it feels.

ESTJ

Efficient, structured and responsible; you excel at organizing resources and following through.

Micro‑practice: Take a small break from your schedule to embrace spontaneity.

ESFP

Energetic, spontaneous and sociable; you live in the moment and bring joy to experiences.

Micro‑practice: Plan a brief solo activity to notice how stillness complements your extroverted energy.

ESTP

Action‑oriented, adaptable and realistic; you solve problems with hands‑on approaches.

Micro‑practice: Pause before making a quick decision to weigh long‑term effects.

For self-awareness only — not a clinical assessment.
